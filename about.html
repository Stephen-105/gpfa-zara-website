Who we are

A community learning centre with purpose.

We make practical computer education welcoming, understandable and accessible to people at every stage of life.

Our story

Learning that strengthens a community

GPFA Zara Computer Centre was created to close the digital skills gap by giving local learners access to computers, patient instruction and relevant training. We serve adults improving their employment skills, parents supporting their families, students preparing for the future and children discovering technology safely.

Our teaching combines clear demonstrations with guided practice, allowing learners to build confidence through doing.

Accessible by design

Courses are free and structured for different levels of experience.

Practical at every step

Lessons focus on real tasks people use at school, work and home.

Focused on progress

Supportive instructors help each learner move forward with confidence.

Our direction

Mission, vision and values

Mission

To provide free, practical and inclusive ICT education that equips community members with useful digital skills.

Vision

A digitally confident community where every person can use technology to learn, connect and create opportunity.

Our values

Access, patience, respect, safety, practical learning and continuous improvement guide everything we do.
